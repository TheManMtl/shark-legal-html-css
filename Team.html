OUR TEAM

The Law Offices Shark bite legal was established in 2019 by former Laval Deputy District Attorney Bryan Madrid.Joined by Attorneys David Howellit, Dustin Shamburg. The Law Offices Shark bite legal represents clients in the Greater Montreal area with offices located in Laval, Longuie, Nuns Island, and NDG. These office locations were strategically selected to properly serve our clients who have matters heard at the Central, West, Harbor, and North Justice Centers. Specializing only in criminal defense, The Law Offices Shark bite legal has provided legal representation to hundreds of clients in the Orange County area who were either charged with a crime or were under investigation for committing a crime. With extensive knowledge and experience in all areas of criminal law and of the court system within the said areas, The Law Offices of Shark bite legal is specially equipped to defend our client’s rights and freedoms.
